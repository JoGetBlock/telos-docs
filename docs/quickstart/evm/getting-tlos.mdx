# Funding Your Wallet

In our previous section you got your wallet set up and linked it to the Telos EVM. In this section we are going to discuss how to obtain TLOS tokens.

The TLOS token is both the governance and resource token of the Telos network and is used for paying gas fees, voting on network wide governance ballots, and managing transaction quota.

## TLOS for Testnet

You can use the [Testnet Faucet](https://app.telos.net/testnet/developers?tab=tlos-evm) to acquire Testnet TLOS. Paste your EVM address and press the `SEND TESTNET EVM TLOS` button. Once completed, you will receive testnet funds to work with and write data to the blockchain.

## TLOS For Mainnet

### On Telos.net Via Ramp, Topper, and Simplex

- Go to [telos.net/buy](https://www.telos.net/buy)
- Choose the network you want to receive TLOS on

<img
    src={require('/static/img/buy-page-1.png').default}
    alt="Buy tlos"
    width="50%"
    height="automatic"
/>

- Scroll down and choose one of the on ramping services
- Enter your payment details
- Complete the KYC(Know Your Customer) identity verification
- Receive your TLOS!!

### Bridging From Other Chains

TLOS is the native coin of the Telos blockchain (like ETH is for Ethereum, BNB is for Binance Smart Chain). TLOS now exists on three blockchains – Telos, Ethereum and BSC. While TLOS is fundamentally used on the Telos blockchain, it is also added on other blockchains for reasons such as liquidity and exposure. They’re pegged 1:1 which means that 1 TLOS on Telos is the same as 1 TLOS on Ethereum (erc20) and BSC (bep20). You can bridge/transfer TLOS between blockchains as per your convenience and needs.

[This article](/quickstart/evm/bridging/) describes the two primary methods used to bridge TLOS.
